Hello and welcome to Plain 1.0. As mentioned earlier, this is a fairly simple template, powered entirely by CSS and XHTML. I am also trying to make as much use of whitespace as possible. Whitespace helps keep the page looking clean and easy on the eye, whilst improving readability also. As with all my templates, a limited number of images have been used, and when they have, they've been to aid the design. This results in an easily customizable template that can be used for an array of different sites.
